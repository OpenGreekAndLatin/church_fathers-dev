Welcome to church_fathers-dev!

About

This repository hosts digital versions of a selection of Church Fathers.

These XML files were produced by data entry company Digital Divide Data (DDD), who corrected and encoded hOCR output generated by Bruce Robertson in accordance with the latest EpiDoc standards. While adequate, more can and should be done to improve the XML (the -dev(elopment) suffix indicates the repository is open to improvement).

Contents

  • Chrysostom: De Sacerdotio (1906) ed. J. Arbuthnot Nairn. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.
  • Cyril: In XII Prophetas, Vol. 1 (1868) ed. Philip Edward Pusey. Oxford: Clarendon.
  • Cyril: In XII Prophetas, Vol. 2 (1868) ed. Philip Edward Pusey. Oxford: Clarendon.
  • Ecclesiasticus: The Greek Text of Codex 248 (1909) ed. John Henry Arthur Hart. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.
  • Gregory of Nazianzus. The Five Theological Orations of Gregory of Nazianzus (1899) ed. Arthur James Mason. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.
  • Pseudo Gregory of Nazianzus. Christus Patiens; Tragoedia Christiana, Quae Inscribi Solet Christos Pasxwn (1885) ed. Johann Georg Brambs. Lipsiae: In Aedibus B.G. Teubneri.
